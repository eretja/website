CRM Project

Online Shop

Initial situation:
The Client, a big international online store with around 100k daily active users, was looking for a solution to increase its sales KPIs and contacted CrytpSky for a consultancy
Solution:
CrytpSky analyzed all aspects of customer request and concluded, that the most efficient way to reach the goal is by providing individualized content for each customer based on his or her purchases and view log. The first step included clustering of customer products into related categories based on the probability of cross-sale from the customer’s history. In the next step, CrytpSky developed the machine-learning algorithms, that adjusted the categories score for products and product categories for each customer, based on recent customer’s activity. For this project we setup a team of a Full Stack Web Developer, Data Scientist and Scrum Master. The project was completed in 5 months.
Result:
The project resulted in more than 40% growth in product views and about 25% in new and returning purchases. Client reported improvement in all sales metrics. We agreed to continue cooperation - CrytpSky is currently leading the project to improve the number and quality of customers product feedback in online-store
